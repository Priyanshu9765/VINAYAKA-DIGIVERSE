Frequently asked questions

1.What is social media marketing and how can it help my business?

Social media marketing is the process of promoting your brand, products, or services on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter). It helps increase brand awareness, drive website traffic, and generate qualified leads for your business.

2. Which platforms do you manage for social media marketing?

We manage all major platforms including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, and Pinterest—customizing strategies based on your target audience and business goals.

3. Do you create content for social media posts?

Yes! We provide complete content creation including graphics, videos, captions, hashtags, and publishing schedules—optimized to engage your audience and boost reach.

4. Can social media marketing help generate real sales?

Absolutely. With the right strategy, targeted ads, and conversion tracking, social media can drive direct traffic to your site and convert followers into paying customers.

5. How do you measure success in social media campaigns?

We track key performance indicators like reach, engagement, click-through rates (CTR), conversions, and ROI using platforms like Meta Business Suite, Google Analytics, and social media insights.

6.Do you offer paid advertising (social media ads) services?

Yes. We create and manage highly targeted ad campaigns on Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and more—optimized for conversions and lead generation.

7. Is social media marketing suitable for small businesses?

Definitely! Social media is one of the most cost-effective ways for small businesses to build visibility, connect with customers, and compete with larger brands online.

8.How soon can I see results from social media marketing?

Results depend on your goals and industry, but most clients begin to see engagement and traffic improvements within 30–60 days of consistent marketing efforts.
